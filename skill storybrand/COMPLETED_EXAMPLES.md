## Example 1: Complete Brand Script – Smile Design Service

**PART 1: A CHARACTER (The Hero)**

Your ideal patient is a successful professional or entrepreneur between 40–60 years old, living or working in Temuco. They've built a life they're proud of—a solid career, strong relationships, maybe they've raised a family or they're at the height of their earning potential. They care about first impressions. They want to present the best version of themselves in every room they enter.

**PART 2: HAS A PROBLEM**

*The Villain*: The passage of time. Despite taking care of themselves, their smile doesn't match the energy and vitality they feel inside. Age shows in their teeth before it shows anywhere else.

*External Problem*: Teeth that have become discolored, worn, or misaligned over time. Their smile doesn't reflect the professional polish they've cultivated in other areas of their life.

*Internal Problem*: Frustration. Self-consciousness. A nagging feeling that their smile is holding them back—not dramatically, but just enough that they think about it before they smile fully in photos or meetings. They hate that feeling.

*Philosophical Problem*: It's wrong that someone who's invested decades in building their success should feel self-conscious about something as visible as their smile. You shouldn't have to choose between smiling freely and maintaining your image.

**PART 3: AND MEETS A GUIDE (San Martín Dent)**

*Empathy*: "We understand this completely. We've worked with hundreds of patients just like you—people who've built incredible lives but whose smiles don't reflect the confidence they feel inside. It's one of the most common things we hear: 'I just wish my smile matched who I am.'"

*Authority*: "San Martín Dent specializes in smile design for exactly your stage of life. We've spent the last [X] years perfecting the art of creating smiles that look natural, timeless, and uniquely yours. Our approach combines the latest aesthetic techniques with genuine artistry—because a beautiful smile should look like you, just your best self. Our patients tell us they notice the difference immediately, and so do the people around them."

**PART 4: WHO GIVES THEM A PLAN**

1. **Envision Your Transformation** – We listen deeply to understand your aesthetic goals, assess your face and personality, and use digital design technology to show you exactly what your smile could look like. You'll see it before we touch a tooth.

2. **Design Your Dream Smile** – Using our proven techniques in porcelain veneers, smile contouring, and color correction, we create a smile that's not just beautiful—it's *authentically you*, enhanced.

3. **Reveal Your Confidence** – You'll experience the moment you catch your reflection and smile without thinking. That's when you know it worked.

**PART 5: AND CALLS THEM TO ACTION**

*Direct CTA*: "Schedule Your Smile Transformation Consultation"
- Location: WhatsApp button, website CTA sections, end of emails, consultation booking page
- Messaging: "Let's create your smile" or "Ready to smile freely?"

*Transitional CTA*: "Download the Smile Design Guide: How to Choose the Right Smile for Your Face"
- Location: Blog posts, email opt-ins, social media
- Messaging: "Not sure if smile design is right for you? This guide answers your biggest questions."

**PART 6: THAT ENDS IN SUCCESS**

*Character Transformation*: 
From **feeling self-conscious about their smile and thinking twice before smiling in photos or professional moments** → To **smiling freely and confidently, with a smile that matches the energy and success they feel inside**.

*Sensory & Emotional Details*:
- They catch their reflection unexpectedly and smile without thinking—that hesitation is gone
- In professional meetings, they're fully present instead of aware of their teeth
- Family photos become moments they actually enjoy, not moments they dread
- Friends and colleagues notice immediately: "Your smile looks amazing—did you do something different?"
- Most importantly: They look in the mirror and think, "That's me. That's who I am." Confidence that shows in everything they do.

**PART 7: AND HELPS THEM AVOID FAILURE**

If you don't address this now:
- Your smile will continue to age while your spirit feels young. That disconnect will grow.
- In five years, you'll wish you'd done this today. Your teeth won't reverse.
- You'll continue having those small moments of self-consciousness—at events, in photos, in the back of your mind.
- You'll watch peers and colleagues who invested in their smile enjoy a confidence you're postponing.
- The longer you wait, the more complex the solution might become (if teeth continue to wear or shift).

## Example 5: Sales Conversation Script – High-Ticket Smile Design

**[Patient walks in for consultation]**

---

**YOU (Guide)**: "Welcome. I'm excited to understand what brought you in today. What's on your mind?"

**PATIENT (Hero shares problem)**: "Well, I've never been totally happy with my smile. I feel like my teeth don't match who I am professionally, and I'm tired of thinking about it."

**YOU (Reflect & Empathize – Part 2)**: "Got it. So you've been feeling like your smile doesn't reflect the professional image you want to project. That's something I hear from almost every patient who sits in that chair. Tell me more—when does it bother you most?"

**PATIENT**: "Mostly in photos and meetings. I catch myself being self-conscious instead of just being present."

**YOU (Demonstrate Authority – Part 3)**: "That makes total sense. Here's what I've learned over [X] years: Your smile is one of the first things people notice about you, and when your smile doesn't match your confidence, it affects how you show up. The good news? This is something we specialize in. We've helped hundreds of patients create smiles that feel authentically *them*, just elevated."

**YOU (Show the Plan – Part 4)**: "Let me show you how we'd approach yours. First, we'd use digital design to show you exactly what your smile could look like—before we touch a thing. You'd see it, approve it, and know exactly what you're getting. Then we'd create it using the latest techniques. And finally, you'd experience that moment when you catch your reflection and smile without thinking."

**[Draw simple diagram: Envision → Design → Reveal]**

**YOU (Clarify Success – Part 6)**: "What that looks like for most patients is they stop thinking about their smile. They just smile. And people notice. They say, 'Did you do something different?' It's subtle, but it changes everything."

**YOU (Name Stakes – Part 7)**: "Now, here's the thing. If we don't do this now, your smile will continue aging, and those moments of self-consciousness will keep adding up. In five years, you'll look at photos from today and think, 'I wish I'd done that then.' But if we do it, you've got decades ahead of you smiling freely."

**YOU (Call to Action – Part 5)**: "So here's what I'd recommend: Let's move forward. We'll start with that digital design so you can see exactly what your smile will look like. Does that feel right to you?"

**PATIENT**: "Yeah, I think so. What's the next step?"

**YOU**: "Perfect. Here's how we'll do it: [Explain next steps, investment, timeline]. You're going to be really happy with this."

---

**Key Elements in This Conversation:**
- ✅ Hero's problem acknowledged (self-consciousness)
- ✅ Empathy demonstrated (hearing this before, understanding the feeling)
- ✅ Authority shown (years of experience, hundreds of satisfied patients)
- ✅ Plan made simple (3 clear steps)
- ✅ Success painted (imagine smiling freely)
- ✅ Stakes named (what happens if you don't)
- ✅ CTA clear (schedule the design phase)

---

Use these examples as templates. Adapt the specific details to your own services, but keep the 7-part structure intact across all your marketing.
