## Full Brand Script Summary

Here's the good news: [San Martín Dent] understands what you're going through [empathy], and we've helped [number] patients just like you [authority]. Here's our simple approach: [Step 1], [Step 2], and [Step 3].

Ready to transform? [Direct CTA]. Not quite ready? [Transitional CTA].

When you work with us, you'll go from [current state] to [desired state], and you'll experience [sensory details]. But if you don't take action, you'll continue to [failure scenario], and in [timeframe], you'll wish you had started today.**
